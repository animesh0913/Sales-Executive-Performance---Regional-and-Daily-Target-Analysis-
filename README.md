                                                      Sales Executive Performance Project
**Overview **
This project focuses on visualizing the performance of sales executives across
different regions in India. The dashboard provides real-time insights into total sales,
target achievement percentages, and comparative analysis of performance across
multiple locations. 

**Introduction** 
Sales performance tracking is a critical component for any organization to assess
productivity, identify top performers, and improve decision-making. This
dashboard consolidates data from multiple regions and visualizes it using charts,
tables, and KPIs for a quick and clear understanding. 

**Objective:**
To monitor and analyze sales executives’ performance across different regions.
To provide a visual representation of sales and target achievements. To help
management identify trends, top performers, and areas needing improvement. 

**Dataset Description:**
Source: Company Sales Records Regions Covered: Bangalore, Chennai,
Delhi, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kolkata, Mumbai, Nagpur, Patna, Pune, Ranchi,
Surat. 
Regions Covered: Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Jaipur,
Kolkata, Mumbai, Nagpur, Patna, Pune, Ranchi, Surat

**Data Fields: **
Sales Executive Name.
Total Sales.
Target Hit % Wise.
Time Period: Daily and Regional-level Sales Data

**Dashboard Features and Visuals** 
Interactive Regional Tabs: Switch between cities to view specific performance
data.
Performance Tables: List of sales executives with total sales and target hit
percentages. 
Bar Charts: Visual representation of sales figures for quick comparison.
Pie Charts: Percentage share of executives’ contributions. 
Line Charts: Trends in target achievement percentages. 
Clean UI: Color-coded regions and dashboards for easy navigation. 

**Key Insights** 
Chennai and other regions show varying performance trends, highlighting
differences in sales potential. 
Some executives significantly outperform others, achieving over 300,000% in
target hit percentage (Dashboard 3). 
Target achievement is highly inconsistent across regions, indicating differences
in sales strategies and market conditions. 

**Insights from Dashboards:**
Dashboard 1: Shows balanced performance with sales ranging from 2500 to
3200 units.
Dashboard 2: Reveals moderate performance, with sales between 120 and 195
units.
Dashboard 3: Exceptional target hit percentages, exceeding 300,000%,
indicating potential errors in targets set or extraordinary performance.
Dashboard 4: Displays high numerical values with closer performance among
executives.

**Conclusion **
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across
multiple regions. It identifies top-performing executives and highlights
discrepancies in target settings. Such a dashboard can be instrumental in
strategic planning and performance evaluation.
